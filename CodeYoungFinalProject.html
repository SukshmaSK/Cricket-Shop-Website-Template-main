Our History




The Eagle Cricket Association started in 2007. Founded by Sir Vangipurapu Venkata Sai Laxman(VVS Laxman), the shop's main mission was to improve the cricketing market. After 3 years of hardcore work, VVS Laxman and his team were able to finally open up their first physical store in Hyderabad, India on May 8th 2010. From there onwards we have been able to open up shops in many cities around the world which consist of Mumbai(India), Bangalore(India), Chennai(India), Melbourne(Australia) and Sydney(Australia).
